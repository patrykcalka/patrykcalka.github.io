Deep Rock Galactic: Survivor

Danger. Darkness. Dwarves.


Dwarves

The Scout is one of four playable classes in DRG Survivor, being unlocked by default.
Scout specializes in mobility and evasion, dodging around enemies while his weapons passively do damage. Scout also has a higher base Critical Damage multiplier than the other classes. His starting weapons consist of the Deepcore GK2, Jury-Rigged Boomstick, Zhukov NUK17, and Cryo Grenade. His class mods are Classic, Recon, and Sharp Shooter, with Classic being unlocked by default.
Starting Stats:
Health: 120
Dodge Chance: 5%
Critical Chance: 5%
Critical Damage: 200%
The Gunner is one of four playable classes in DRG Survivor, unlocked by reaching Player Rank 3.
Gunner's kit consists almost entirely of projectile weapons, with a few fire weapons alongside for more AOE damage. His starting weapons consist of the "Lead Storm" Powered Minigun, "Bulldog" Heavy Revolver, and Incendiary Grenade, making them the only class to not begin with four weapons. His class mods are Weapons Specialist, Juggernaut, and Heavy Gunner, with Weapons Specialist being available to the player upon unlocking the class for the first time.
Starting Stats:
Health: 160
Armor: 5%
Critical Chance: 5%
Critical Damage: 150%
The Engineer is one of four playable characters in DRG Survivor, unlocking when reaching Player Rank 5.
Engineer focuses around area control and lockdown gameplay. This makes him ideal for holding a single area, but struggles during exploration and resourcing. His starting weapons consist of the LMG Gun Platform, "Warthog" Auto 210, "Stubby" Voltaic SMG, and Hi-Volt Thunderbird. His class mods are Maintenance Worker, Tinkerer, and Demolitionist, with Maintenance Worker being unlocked by default.
Starting Stats:
Health: 130
+XP Gain: 5%
Critical Chance: 5%
Critical Damage: 150%
The Driller is one of four playable characters in DRG Survivor, unlocking at Player Rank 7.
Driller boasts the fastest mining speed in the game, and the most elemental weapons of any class. His starting weapons are the CRSPR Flamethrower, Subata 120, High Explosive Grenade, and Krakatoa Sentinel. His class mods are Foreman, Interrogator, and Strong Armed, with Foreman being unlocked by default.
Starting Stats:
Health: 145
Critical Chance: 5%
Critical Damage: 150%
Mining Speed: +20%
